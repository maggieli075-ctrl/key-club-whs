Volunteer or Request for an Event

Volunteer events will always be posted on the VolunteerSignup. To register for an event, enter your name, phone number, and email in the case that the event managers want to contact you to provide more information, to cancel, to provide updates, etc. Many events usually have a maximum number of volunteers.

Volunteer
How It Works
  1. Officers update the events every few weeks
  2. Visit VolunteerSignup to register with your name, phone number, and email
  3. Event coordinators may contact you with more information, cancellation, etc.
  4. Most events have volunteer limits and fill up quickly
